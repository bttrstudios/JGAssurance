What We Offer

Coverage for Every Stage of Life

As an independent broker, Jesse works with all major carriers to find the plan that fits your needs — not the other way around.

Medicare Advantage Plans

All-in-one Medicare coverage bundling hospital, medical, and often prescription drug benefits through private carriers.

Medicare Supplement (Medigap)

Fill the gaps in Original Medicare coverage — including copays, coinsurance, and deductibles — with a standardized supplement plan.

ACA / Marketplace Coverage

Navigate the Affordable Care Act marketplace with expert guidance to find the right plan and maximize your available subsidies.

Social Security Optimization

As a Registered Social Security Analyst (RSSA®), Jesse helps you determine the ideal time and strategy to maximize your lifetime benefits.

Prescription Drug Plans (Part D)

Compare standalone Medicare Part D plans to ensure your medications are covered at the lowest possible out-of-pocket cost.

Dental, Vision & Hearing Plans

Supplemental coverage for dental, vision, and hearing needs — benefits often missing from standard Medicare plans.
